Definitions

Original Data

CASE & DEATH

Case Density: The number of cases per 100k population calculated using a 7-day rolling average.

Cases: Cumulative confirmed or suspected cases.

Deaths: Cumulative deaths that are suspected or confirmed to have been caused by COVID-19.

New Cases: New confirmed or suspected cases.

New Deaths: New confirmed or suspected deaths caused by COVID-19.

VACCINATION

Vaccine Distributed: Number of vaccine doses distributed.

Vaccine Initiated Ratio: Ratio of population that has initiated vaccination.

Vaccine Completed Ratio: Ratio of population that has completed vaccination.

ICU INFORMATION

ICU Beds: Total ICU beds in a state.

ICU Capacity Ratio: Ratio of staffed intensive care unit (ICU) beds that are currently in use.

CurrentIcuCovid: Number of COVID patients currently in ICU.

CurrentIcuNonCovid: Number of non-COVID patients currently in ICU.
